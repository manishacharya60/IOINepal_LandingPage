About Us

We are CSPrep.org, an organization dedicated to transforming Nepal's tech education landscape. Our mission is to raise awareness, provide necessary resources, and mentorship to inspire young tech enthusiasts in Nepal. Our project, the Nepal IOI Project, aims to enhance student engagement in competitive programming. With your help, we aspire to mark Nepal's presence in the International Olympiad in Informatics..

Mentorship program

Our mentorship program is a cornerstone of our initiative. Industry professionals and college mentors from the U.S. volunteer their time to guide, inspire, and foster a nurturing learning environment for our aspiring programmers.
